About Arellano University


Arellano University Juan Sumulong Campus is an esteemed educational institution located in the Philippines. Named after the renowned Filipino nationalist, Juan Sumulong, this campus is part of the respected Arellano University system. It is known for its commitment to providing quality education and a wide range of academic programs, catering to the diverse needs and aspirations of its students. With a rich history and a dedication to excellence, Arellano University Juan Sumulong Campus stands as a beacon of learning and opportunity in the heart of the Philippines.
